The city has long been improving its online services. Now they’re not just an additional convenience — they have become a necessity instead. We have created an online medium. A huge benefit of the online cultural platform is that access to it and the broadcasts of all events will always be free. You will be able to view video versions of stage plays, concerts, exhibitions and film premieres, take virtual tours to museums and parks and enjoy other exciting events.

Have an evening of culture at home: look through the event guide and choose what you think is worth seeing or listening to, or reading. By not going on a cultural outing, you will save some cash that you will be able to use later to have fun.

In any case, theatres, museums, concerts, performances, interesting lectures, and tours provided by the mos.ru online cultural platform will stay with you forever!
