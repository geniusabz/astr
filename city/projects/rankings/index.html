Moscow Accomplishments:
international awards and rankings
Moscow's accomplishments in various fields — from transport to heritage restoration — are awarded prestigious Russian and international prizes. Rankings topped by the Russian capital speak for the progress, too.

Moscow awards
Annually, Moscow receives awards at the international and Russian competitions for urban redevelopment. Experts mentioned the launch of the Moscow Central Circle, the quality of electronic public services and the development of Magistral surface transport network. Moscow’s heritage preservation programs are acclaimed as one of the most effective in the world. New urban spaces created in the Russian capital win international awards.
