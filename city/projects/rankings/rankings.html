Moscow in the rankings
Moscow has been changing and has already entered the rankings of the world’s most advanced capitals: it has joined the top 5 best cities in the world and is the third of the world’s megacities for the transport infrastructure development. Moscow is in for more victories in the coming years, as it has moved almost 50 points up in the ranking of the most promising centres of economic growth in one year, entered the top 7 for the development of financial technologies in Europe and the top 10 for the startup ecosystem development in the world.
